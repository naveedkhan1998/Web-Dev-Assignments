Welcome to the Western University Campus Recreation Center

State-of-the-Art Facilities

The Western University Campus Recreation Center is a cutting-edge facility dedicated to enhancing the health and wellness of the student community. The center boasts an impressive array of modern amenities designed to cater to your fitness and recreational needs.

The state-of-the-art gym features top-of-the-line exercise equipment, including cardio machines, weightlifting stations, and group fitness studios. Spacious courts are available for various sports like basketball, volleyball, and racquetball, ensuring there's something for everyone.

Exciting Activities for All Students

As a current student, you have the privilege of free access to the Western University Campus Recreation Center. A wide range of activities is offered to cater to your interests and fitness levels, creating a vibrant and inclusive environment.

You can break a sweat in the well-equipped fitness center, where certified trainers are available to help you achieve your fitness goals. Join in the fun with intramural sports leagues, or simply enjoy a friendly game of table tennis in the recreational area. For those seeking relaxation, the indoor swimming pool provides the perfect escape.

The Western University Campus Recreation Center is committed to fostering a welcoming atmosphere that encourages community engagement and personal growth. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or just looking for a place to unwind, the center is your hub for a healthy and active student life.
